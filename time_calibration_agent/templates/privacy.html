Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Untangle ("we", "us", "our") is a day-planning tool that helps you schedule and manage your time. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Planning input: The text you enter to describe your day (tasks, deadlines, context). This is sent to OpenAI to generate your schedule.
  • Session data: Your generated plans are stored server-side, keyed to an anonymous session ID stored in a browser cookie. No account or email is required.
  • Google Calendar tokens: If you connect Google Calendar, we store OAuth tokens (access token + refresh token) on the server, scoped only to creating calendar events. We never read your existing calendar data.
  • Usage analytics: Anonymous page-view and event data via Google Analytics 4 (no personally identifiable information).

2. How We Use Google Calendar Data

When you click "Add to Google Calendar", Untangle uses the Google Calendar API with the calendar.events scope to:

  • Create calendar events for each time block in your approved plan.

We do not read, modify, or delete any existing events on your calendar. We do not share your calendar data with any third party.

Untangle's use of Google user data complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data. Your planning text is transmitted to OpenAI solely to generate schedule outputs, subject to OpenAI's privacy policy.

4. Data Retention

Session data (your plans) is stored on our servers indefinitely to support session continuity, but is keyed to an anonymous session cookie. You can start fresh at any time, which creates a new anonymous session. Google Calendar tokens are stored until you revoke access via your Google Account settings.

5. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. OAuth tokens are stored server-side in per-user directories and are never exposed to other users.

6. Children's Privacy

Untangle is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.

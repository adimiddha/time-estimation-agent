Untangle

AI-powered day planning that gets you unstuck.

Untangle turns a brain dump of tasks and commitments into a realistic, time-blocked schedule for your day — in seconds. Just tell it what's on your plate, and it figures out the rest.

What it does

  • Takes your task list, deadlines, and energy level as plain text
  • Builds a realistic time-blocked schedule using AI
  • Replans on the fly when your day changes
  • Pushes your plan directly to Google Calendar with one click
  • Works on desktop and mobile — no account required

How your data is used

Your planning text is sent to OpenAI to generate your schedule. If you connect Google Calendar, Untangle uses the calendar.events scope to create events from your plan — it never reads or modifies your existing calendar data.

No personal account is required. Sessions are keyed to an anonymous browser cookie.
